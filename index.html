Impact Alert is comming to your phone soon!

The very first hardware-less alert for motorcycle and bicycle accidents is here!. If you are out riding alone, or even with friends, Impact Alert is a great security tool for detecting and alerting your friends and family in case you get involved in an accident.

No more complicated sensors.

Impact Alert works within your smartphone! No need for complicated and overpriced sensors. Impact Alert uses the accelerometers that come with your smartphone to detect accidents.

Fully customizable, and free!.

Impact Alert lets you choose who to contact and through what means. SMS, calls, e-mail. In order to maximize the chances that someone sees your distress call, Impact Alert can contact your dear ones. All of this for the price of nothing. With a free version available, you can add extra safety to your rides instantly.
